What We Do

Commercial Locksmith Services

Hardware service built around the real needs of commercial buildings, offices, retail spaces, and multi-tenant properties.

Commercial Lever Locks

Installation, repair, and replacement of standard commercial grade lever locks. The hardware most businesses deal with daily on office doors, suite entries, and interior passages.

Panic Hardware

Exit device installation and repair for storefront doors, fire exits, and commercial entries. Push bars, crash bars, and rim exit devices serviced to meet code requirements.

Rekeying & Lock Changes

Rekey existing hardware during tenant turnover or employee changes. Full lock change-outs when needed. Fast turnaround so your building stays secure without downtime.

Door Hardware Repairs

Troubleshooting and repair of closers, hinges, strikes, latch mechanisms, and alignment issues on commercial hollow metal and aluminum storefront doors.

Master Key Systems

Design and implementation of master key systems for multi-tenant buildings, office complexes, and commercial properties. Organized access control without electronic overhead.

Business Locksmith Service

General commercial locksmith support for offices, retail locations, churches, schools, and commercial facilities. One call or text handles the full scope of your door hardware needs.

Our Focus

Built for Commercial Doors

We work on the real-world hardware that commercial buildings depend on, not residential deadbolts or decorative handles.

Most locksmith companies split their focus between houses, cars, and businesses. We concentrate on commercial door hardware because that is what property managers, office managers, and business owners actually need help with.

From a sticking lever lock on a suite door to a panic bar that will not latch on a fire exit, the problems are specific and the solutions require hands-on hardware knowledge. We handle the standard commercial-grade hardware found in offices, retail storefronts, churches, schools, and multi-tenant properties throughout the Tampa area.
